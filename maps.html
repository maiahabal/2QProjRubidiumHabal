MAPS

There are currently seven different maps available to players in VALORANT.
These are Bind, Haven, Split, Ascent, Icebox, Breeze, and Fracture.

Bind

This map is known for its teleporters that can carry agents, utilities, and weapons.

Haven

This map is known for having three sites, whereas all other maps have only two.

Split

Split's dynamic element is characterized by having elevated ground separating the two sites. These elevated grounds (called heaven in game) are accessed through ropes.

Ascent

This map features irreversible bomb doors on both sites. These doors can be used by both defenders and attackers to their own advantage.

Icebox

Aside from the many angles to consider in this map, it features rope ascenders and ziplines for more dynamic movements.

Breeze

This map is the biggest and most spacious map in valorant. It has ropes, doors, and a one-way chute.

Fracture

Enemies can come from either side of the map while defenders spawn in the middle, as opposed to having defenders spawn on one side and attackers on the other.
